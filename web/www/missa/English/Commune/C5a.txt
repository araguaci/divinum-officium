[Name]
Commune Confessoris non pontificis; In medio

[Rule]
Gloria

[Secreta1]
May the pious prayer of holy N. thy Confessor and illustrious Doctor be not wanting to us,~
O Lord, but make our offerings acceptable to thee and ever win for us thy mercy.
$Per Dominum.


[Postcommunio1]
! Pro Doctore non Pontifice.
May blessed N. thy Confessor and illustrious Doctor intercede for us,~
O Lord, that this thy sacrifice may obtain for us salvation.
$Per Dominum
